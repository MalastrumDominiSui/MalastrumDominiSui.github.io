About

"To thine own self be true, for it must follow as dost the night the day, that canst not then be false to any man." - Shakespeare ("Hamlet") inspired by Socrates ("Know thyself", quoting Pythia, the Oracle of Delphi)

Welcome to my new website! In 2016 I was ecstatic to join Omaha Code School. This personal web-site is the result of my newest and growing skill set: web-development. I'm using it as a platform to promote my own growth, my skills, business ventures, and anything else I can think of. I'm having a lot of fun with it.

I have both a logical side, and a creative side. My Myers-Briggs has always pinned me as an ENFP, but this has surprised people before. I always maintain that personality tests don't determine personality, but rather us who self-determine the results.

I'm a worker, a dreamer, and a growing entrepeneur. Since 2015 I have self-taught myself the nuances of 3D printing and design. I brainstormed with my friend John and the result was Terrainify: high-quality, handcrafted terrain for table-top gamers. I designed all the pieces, and we've sold over almost 200 pieces in less than a year. It's been a blast! Check it out here:

Terrainify

We just recently released "Red Rock", for anyone looking to reproduce the exotic barren look of a Martian wasteland.


If you need help with either Web Development, or with a custom 3D printing idea, feel free to Contact Me.
